# BeingCamp

## What it is

A creative-community ecosystem app for a real physical space in Kerala, India.
Members earn and spend a closed-loop currency (BeingCoin), take briefs from a
work marketplace with database-escrowed milestones, publish case studies to a
shared Showcase, and check into physical zones. One React codebase ships as
mobile web / installable PWA / native shells (Capacitor) and a desktop web app.

## Audience

Creative professionals and clients in Kerala (designers, filmmakers, makers,
brand owners), plus the founder operating the space (admin tooling).

## Register

product — design SERVES the product. Data-first surfaces: wallet, ledger,
projects, escrow, catalog, leaderboard. The money screens must feel
trustworthy before they feel expressive.

## Platform

web (responsive desktop shell + phone full-bleed app; native shells wrap the
same build).

## Voice

Confident, warm, concise. Kerala personality in microcopy, not in noise.
Numbers speak first; labels stay small and quiet.
